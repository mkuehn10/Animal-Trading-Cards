Domestic Cat

The average cat sleeps 16-18 hours per day.

  • Conservation Status : Domesticated
  • Binomial Name : Felis catus
  • Average Lifespan : 15 years
  • Awesomeness Level : Very High

Domestic cats are small, furry, and cute. They are playful for the 6-8 hours per day when they are not sleeping. Cats tend to breed a lot, so it is important to spay and neuter your pet cats to help control the pet population.
